## 1. Introduction

As part of my internship, I was assigned the task of conducting research
on User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) designs of various
AI-based tools, Agentic AI platforms, and AI Copilot systems.

The purpose of this task is to understand how modern digital platforms
design their interfaces to attract users, generate leads, and convert
visitors into customers. This research also focuses on improving content
structure, navigation flow, and overall user experience.

## 6. Conclusion

This UI/UX research analyzed eight AI-based platforms to understand
modern design practices.

Key learnings include: - Clean layouts improve readability\
- Proper spacing enhances visual balance\
- Interactive elements increase engagement\
- Clear CTAs improve conversions\
- Organized structure improves usability

These insights can be applied to design more user-friendly and
professional AI platforms.
